WELCOME TO CPA COLLEGE!

Welcome to CPA College! Established on July 19, 1974, and affiliated with Madurai Kamaraj University, we embody the values of LIGHT, KNOWLEDGE, and GROWTH. Committed to providing quality education, our focus is on empowering deserving students in Bodinayakanur and beyond. As part of the Cardamom Planters' Association, we strive to illuminate minds, instill knowledge, and foster holistic growth. Join us in a transformative educational journey where we cultivate a passion for learning and inspire personal development. Welcome to a community dedicated to your success at CPA College!
